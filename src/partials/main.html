Pumpkin Master

Where Magic Flickers, and Fortune Awakens.

Welcome to a place where night never ends and pumpkins never die. Pumpkin Master is your entry into a forgotten festival of mischief and mystery, where every spin might summon symbols of riches—or shadows. You don’t play this game...
it plays you.

Not All Spinning Wheels Belong to Fortune Tellers

Long ago, under a crimson moon, a secret celebration began deep in the woods. The Pumpkin Master—half trickster, half gatekeeper—appeared, offering gold to those brave enough to spin his haunted wheel.

Now, you too are invited to the midnight carnival.
The wheel is alive. The prizes are real. The risks? Gloriously unknown.

This is Halloween without costumes. This is magic without rules. This is Pumpkin Master.

What Powers the Ritual

Every element of Pumpkin Master carries a story, and every feature carries a twist.

Core Features:

Each mechanic is designed to unsettle, reward, and surprise—even after hundreds of spins.

  • Jack-o’-Wilds: 

    Glowing pumpkins substitute with spectral flare, multiplying your line wins in eerie silence.

  • Phantom Re-Spin:

    When the ghosts rise, the reels re-spin without cost—but with cursed volatility.

  • Lantern Bonus:

    Collect glowing lanterns to unlock a special wheel surrounded by whispering trees and golden vines.

  • Witch’s Pattern:

    Random overlays cast by a hidden witch alter symbols into their highest-paying forms.

Art That Breathes, Sound That Lurks

This isn’t just a spooky background. The forest is alive. The pumpkins blink. The wind howls in stereo.

Pumpkin Master is a masterclass in atmospheric game design—fusing responsive animations, soft particle trails, layered fog, and reactive ambient sounds that change depending on your wins.

It’s not just what you see—it’s what you feel behind your screen.
